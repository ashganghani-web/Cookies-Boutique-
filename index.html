Our Story

More than fashion —
a movement.

My name is Ashgan. Seven years ago, I was diagnosed with schizophrenia. The medication that helped my mind changed my body — I went from 65kg to 95kg.

I've always loved fashion. But suddenly, every piece I fell in love with didn't fit. Every fitting room became a reminder of something I didn't choose.

I know that feeling. The frustration of loving style but not finding your size. Of being curvy, beautiful, and completely overlooked by fashion.

That's why I created Cookies Boutique — a space where curvy women don't just find clothes, they find confidence. Because you deserve to feel beautiful — exactly as you are. 🤍
